    📄 Privacy Policy

     

    What We Collect: Your name, Facebook username, location, email (if provided), availability, and any extra notes submitted through our forms.

     

    Why We Collect It: To ensure accurate bookings, provide quality service, and to follow up on inquiries or feedback.

     

    How It’s Used: We use your info only for Reuben’s Odd Jobs services. We will never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

     

    Data Security: Your data is securely handled through EmailJS, a trusted service for form submission. We don’t store your info in databases.

     

    Control Over Your Data: You can request to delete your data at any time by contacting Reuben directly via Messenger or through the booking confirmation email.

     

    Cookies: We do not use cookies. No tracking tech is installed on this site.

     

    Children’s Privacy: We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years old.
